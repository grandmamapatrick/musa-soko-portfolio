MORTICA

2023 Title Sequence 1:52 min 9:16

The client wanted something nostalgic and warm. This is a spec piece exploring golden hour lighting and the specific feeling of summer storms — that moment where the air is heavy before the sky breaks.

Made in 3 days as a personal challenge to work faster without sacrificing atmosphere. The rain was built from a mix of practical footage and digital compositing.

Brief
"Create something that feels like memory and captures the warmth of summer evenings when the rain hits hot pavement and...
the whole world smells like wet earth. The piece should feel both nostalgic and present — like remembering a dream while you're still in it. Evoke golden hour light, the sound of rain on leaves, and that specific feeling of being warm inside while it's storming outside."
