MORTICA came together the second exams were over — all that pent-up energy finally let loose. A music video and full motion-graphics piece built as a deliberate beautiful mess: graffiti, spray-paint splashes and torn textures crashing into soft flora, like something refreshing pushing up through concrete.

Vibrant to the point of reckless. Every rule of "clean" motion design got broken on purpose — and it's still one of my favourites.
