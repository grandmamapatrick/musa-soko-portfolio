NOTE: One of the last pieces I made on my old ThinkCentre before it took a long hiatus. Still works perfectly, I just don't lean on it much these days. A full process breakdown of how this was made is coming soon.

Cuvée was a visual direction I wanted to present to the company, a concept for a new look. It was a concept piece.

For the look, I leaned on something I'd noticed: sommeliers and a lot of wine brands gravitate toward black and white. So I built the foundation in monochrome, then put a twist on it. Gold accents with a grungy texture, like peeling the foil off a champagne bottle, and a deep purple underneath. The gold and purple were the luxury cues, purple especially, since historically only the rich and royal got to wear it.
