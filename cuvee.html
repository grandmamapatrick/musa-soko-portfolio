NOTE: One of the last pieces made on my old ThinkCentre PC before it gave out. RIP. A full process breakdown of how this was made is coming soon.

I made this video to showcase a potential new visual direction for the company. It was a concept piece. I took inspiration from wine connoisseurs and sommeliers, who often gravitate toward black and white. So I built the foundation in monochrome, then added subtle gold accents with a grungy texture, like peeling the foil off a champagne bottle

The purple came from the historical association between wine drinkers and royalty (royalty wore purple). So there's a subtle nod to that throughout.
