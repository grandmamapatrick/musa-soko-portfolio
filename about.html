Hi! My name is Musa, and I love making stuff that makes people feel something, through motion and video editing.

I’m a 24-year-old South African motion designer and video editor based in Johannesburg. I’ve been working in the motion design and video editing industry for about four years now, with companies both abroad and here at home.

I love learning new things. I’m always on a new course, picking up a new technique, honing my skills, or just experimenting. And in my free time, I run around, play games, and draw a little.
