I LIKE MAKING THINGS
I LIKE LEARNING NEW THINGS THINGS

Novus F is a personal project of mine. "Novus" is Latin for new, and the F could really mean anything: frame, film, form you name it. So it can be... whatever. It began as a short little intro, but I kept throwing new ideas at it that i wanted to impliment, so it grew into a motion piece, way bigger than I planned.

From there it was mostly planning. I pulled a ton of references for the shapes and the color palette, then sketched out storyboards for a few key frames to figure out how it all fit together.

This was the project where I got to try everything I'd been sitting on. Some of those bits were fun, some were a challenge to pull off, but really worth it.
