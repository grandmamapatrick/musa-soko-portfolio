FAQ

How long will it take?
Depends on the project. Short form stuff usually takes 1-2 days. Long form about a week. If you need graphics packages like lower thirds, transitions, intros well that has its own timeline. But honestly? We'll know more once we talk through what you actually need. That's when I can give you an actual answer.
What kind of projects do you love most?
I like making things move. Motion graphics, animation just anything where I get to bring stuff to life. Video editing is cool too, don't get me wrong. But I wanna edit at that psychiatrist level, the kind where you're invoking something in people, you know? like Oppenheimer, Severance, or Kinds of Kindness type of stuff.
I have an idea but I'm not sure it's fully formed. Can I still reach out?
Yeah, absolutely. Everything starts somewhere. We'll talk it through, see what's there, and figure it out together.
How do you handle feedback?
Fine. If something's not working, just tell me and I'll fix it. I don't take it personally. Tell me what you want different and we'll move forward.
How often will you update me?
Regular check-ins. Not too much, just consistent enough so you're never guessing where things are at. Use whatever works for you: Zulip, Slack, email, etc. I'll be there.
What's your process like?
We talk first. You tell me what you need, even if it's vague it's fine. I'll put together some references, we'll hop on a call, and you tell me what's working. Then I get to work. Rough draft, feedback, revisions if needed, final. Simple.
Is it true that you accept Twinkies as a form of payment?
Uh, what!? No! It was just the one time!
